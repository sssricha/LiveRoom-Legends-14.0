How to Play?

You can start the program and customize your room. You can press "Start" in Lobby or use a Redeem Code. After that, Take a screenshot and Upload it. You also have a chance of getting a redeem code. If you get a reedem code, you are also helping others to create their room. Also you can be in the "Leaderboard Championship". Your Username will be Displayed. You can Interact with yur Character by Dragging your mouse-pointer.


What's in it for me?

Well... As mentioned, You also have a chance of getting a redeem code. If you get a reedem code, you are also helping others to create their room. Also you can be in the "Leaderboard Championship". Your Username will be Displayed.


What about the Leaderboard?

When you create a perfect room. You will get redeem codes of 10,000 RoomCoins which is displayed. You are also helping others to get 10,000 RoomCoins which will expire after a month exactly. The first three players will be mentioned here.


What did we use for the Authentication and The game development?

We used Scratch. Scratch is a programming language and an online community where everyone can program and share interactive media such as games with people from all over the world. Also, Padlet. And most of all... Google Forms where you can submit forms... Obviously, and recieve data.
